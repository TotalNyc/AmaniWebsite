About THE AMANI

THE AMANI at 1353 Castle Hill Avenue offers a perfect blend of comfort, style, and convenience in the vibrant Castle Hill neighborhood of the Bronx. Our thoughtfully designed residences feature premium finishes and modern amenities to enhance your lifestyle.

Whether you're looking for a cozy studio, a spacious one-bedroom, or a comfortable two-bedroom apartment, THE AMANI has the perfect home for you. Each residence features contemporary design elements including stainless steel appliances, quartz countertops, luxury vinyl plank flooring, and spacious layouts.

Located just minutes from public transportation, shopping, dining, and recreational areas, THE AMANI gives you access to everything the vibrant Bronx community has to offer while providing a peaceful retreat to call home.

Neighborhood

Discover Castle Hill

Castle Hill is a vibrant neighborhood in the southeast section of the Bronx, known for its close-knit community and convenient amenities. Living at THE AMANI puts you in the center of all that this dynamic area has to offer.

The neighborhood features a perfect blend of urban convenience and residential charm, with excellent transportation options, diverse dining choices, and recreational facilities nearby.
